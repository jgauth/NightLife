FAQ

    Q: Who organizes and or creates the parties hosted on the NightLife web application?
    A: Any user or host that signs up on the nightlife web application may host a party or event.

    Q: Can professors or law enforcement find out about these parties?
    A: No, only NightLife users can discover the parties posted on the web application.

    Q: Do fraternities exclusively host these parties?
    A: No. Anyone can host an event on NightLife.

    Q: Are these parties exclusively for University of Oregon students?
    A: Yes. At this time, NightLife is limited to the University of Oregon students and Eugene area.

    Q: Is there a certain age limitation for each party listed on NightLife?
    A: Any applicable age restrictions will be listed in the party description.

    Q: Does the nighlight application work on mobile device web browsers?
    A: Yes nightlife is available on moblie devices through web browser access.

    Q: I believe someone is misusing NightLife. What Should I do?
    A: If any abuse or misuse of NightLife's features is suspected please promptly contact us
    by emailing nightlifeassistance@gmail.com with a description of the issue encountered.
